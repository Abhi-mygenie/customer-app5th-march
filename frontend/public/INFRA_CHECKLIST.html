5. What NOT to conclude

  • ❌ Cannot say CRM is broken — its out log shows no errors, but that log doesn't include DB failures. Need the error log.
  • ❌ Cannot say CRM caused the primary loss — nothing in either file points at CRM.
  • ❌ Cannot say every Customer App request fails during Atlas failover — the frontend has a localStorage config cache, so returning users may not notice. Only first-time landings hit the 500.
  • ❌ Cannot say the Atlas cluster is chronically unhealthy — evidence is one ~30s window with two traces. It may already have self-healed.
  • ✅ Can say: the Customer App backend has no resilience to a routine MongoDB failover, and the failing endpoint sits on the customer's critical path.
